50% off your total food bill!

All diners in a party can eat and receive a 50% discount on their food, even with just one AppetitCard member in the party. However, please do check with the individual restaurant for the maximum numbers permitted per AppetitCard booking.

2 meals for the price of 1!

2 meals for the price of 1 is available for two people only and is across all courses ordered; the cheapest or equivalent dish of each course being free. Side dishes and extras are not included.
